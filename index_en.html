About Three Days & Three Nights

"Three Days & Three Nights" Board Game Studio was founded in Shanghai in 2025 by a group of board game enthusiasts, game designers and developers. Our mission is to bring the joy of classic board games to more people while expanding the possibilities of board games using modern technology.

We believe that board games are not just a form of entertainment, but also a bridge connecting people. Whether it's face-to-face physical board games or online games through the APP, the core value lies in bringing joy and promoting communication.
